Privacy Policy for Hishab Potro

Effective Date: 19 January 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Hishab Potro ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

By using the Hishab Potro app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our service:

Information You Provide Directly

  • Customer Information: When you add a customer, we collect and store the customer's name and, optionally, their phone number. This information is stored locally on your device.
  • Transaction Information: We collect and store financial transaction details that you manually enter, including amounts, descriptions, dates, and due dates. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Shop Name: We store the shop or business name you provide in the app settings.

Information Collected with Your Permission

  • Contact Information: With your explicit permission, we may access your device contacts to help you add customers. Only the selected contact’s name and phone number are used. We do not store or upload your full contact list.
  • Profile Picture: With your permission, you may select an image from your device gallery to use as a profile picture. This image is stored locally on your device.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in using Google, we receive your name and email address from Google for identification purposes within the app.
  • Usage Data (Firebase Analytics): We collect information about how the app is used, such as feature interactions and session duration. This data does not directly identify you and is used to improve app functionality and performance.
  • Advertising Data (Google AdMob): The app displays ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information, including the advertising ID, to show relevant ads. Learn more at: Google Advertising Policies .

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain core app features
  • To manage customers and transaction records
  • To personalize your experience
  • To enable backup and restore functionality
  • To analyze usage and improve the app
  • To send reminders and notifications
  • To support the app through advertising

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All financial records, customer data, and images are stored locally on your device using an SQLite database.
  • Cloud Backup: If you enable Google Drive backup, an encrypted copy of your data is stored in a private folder within your own Google Drive account. We do not have access to this data. Encryption keys remain on your device.

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In & Google Drive API: Subject to the Google Privacy Policy .
  • Google AdMob: Advertising Policies
  • Firebase Analytics & Remote Config: Firebase Privacy Information

5. Data Deletion

  • Local Data: Uninstalling the app deletes all locally stored data.
  • Cloud Backup: Backup data can be deleted manually from your Google Drive account.

6. Children’s Privacy

Hishab Potro is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page by updating the effective date.
