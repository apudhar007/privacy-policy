Privacy Policy

How Hishab Potro collects, uses, and protects your data

1 Introduction

Welcome to Hishab Potro ("the App"), a digital ledger application designed to help small business owners and shopkeepers manage their financial records digitally. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our App.

By downloading, installing, or using Hishab Potro, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App immediately.

This policy applies to all users of the Hishab Potro mobile application, available on Android devices.

2 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Shop Name and profile picture (used for branding on receipts and documents)
  • Customer names and phone numbers entered into the ledger
  • Financial transaction data including amounts, dates, notes, and due dates
  • PIN code (stored locally and encrypted on your device only)
  • Google account information (used solely for Google Drive backup authentication)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Firebase Authentication data: user ID, email address, login timestamps, and device identifiers
  • App usage data for account management and security purposes
  • Device information such as operating system version and app version

2.3 Information from Third-Party Sources

  • Contact information accessed from your phone's contact list — only when you explicitly choose to import a contact
  • Google Drive metadata — only the App's own backup files within the restricted App Data folder

3 How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core App Functionality

  • To display, calculate, and manage your digital ledger (Hishab Khata) accurately
  • To generate professional PDF receipts and cash memos with your shop branding
  • To enable sharing of receipts and balance reminders via WhatsApp, IMO, or SMS
  • To set and send due date payment reminders and notifications

3.2 Data Backup & Sync

  • To automatically back up your ledger data to your own Google Drive (App Data folder only)
  • To restore your data if you change or reset your device

3.3 Security & Account Management

  • To authenticate your identity via Firebase and maintain account security
  • To enforce PIN protection for your ledger
  • To allow the app administrator to suspend or ban accounts that violate our Terms of Service

3.4 Communication

  • To allow one-tap calling and SMS to your customers directly from the App
  • To deliver in-app notices including suspension notices with support contact details

4 Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your ledger data is primarily stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage. This ensures your data remains accessible even without an internet connection.

4.2 Google Drive Backup

When you enable Google Drive Sync, your data is backed up to the App Data folder on your personal Google Drive account. This folder is private — only the Hishab Potro app can access it. We do not have access to your Google Drive files outside of this restricted folder.

4.3 Firebase Cloud Services

We use Google Firebase for user authentication and account management. Firebase stores your user ID, email address, and login history on secure Google servers. Firebase is compliant with international data protection standards including GDPR.

4.4 Security Measures

  • PIN-based lock screen to prevent unauthorized physical access to your ledger
  • Encrypted local data serialization for automatic backups
  • Firebase Authentication for secure cloud identity verification
  • We do not store your PIN on our servers — it is kept locally on your device only

5 Data Sharing & Disclosure

🔒 We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We only share data in the following limited circumstances:
  • Google Firebase: For authentication, user management, and security enforcement
  • Google Drive: For your own personal backup — data goes to your account, not ours
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Safety & Fraud Prevention: To protect the rights, property, or safety of users or the public

We do not display advertisements and do not share your data with any advertising networks.

6 App Permissions

Hishab Potro requests the following device permissions. All permissions are used solely for the stated purpose:

READ_CONTACTS Import customer names and numbers from your phone's contact list
CALL_PHONE Enable one-tap calling to customers from within the App
SEND_SMS Send balance reminder SMS messages directly to customers
CAMERA / STORAGE Upload your shop profile picture for receipt branding
INTERNET Firebase authentication and Google Drive backup/sync
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Deliver due date reminders and payment alerts

You may deny optional permissions. Doing so will disable the corresponding feature but will not affect the core ledger functionality.

7 Children's Privacy

Hishab Potro is intended for use by adults and business owners. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

8 Your Rights & Choices

As a user of Hishab Potro, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data at any time within the App
  • Correction: You can edit or update any customer or transaction record at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete individual transactions, customers, or your entire account data from within the App. Soft-deleted data can be restored using the Undo feature within a limited time window
  • Data Portability: Your data is stored in standard formats on your own device and Google Drive
  • Revoke Backup Access: You can disconnect Google Drive access at any time via your Google account settings
  • Account Deletion: You may contact us to request complete deletion of your Firebase account and associated data

9 Account Suspension Policy

The Hishab Potro developer reserves the right to suspend or permanently ban accounts found in violation of our Terms of Service, including:

  • Fraudulent or abusive use of the application
  • Attempts to reverse-engineer, hack, or manipulate the app or its data
  • Violation of applicable laws or regulations

Suspended users will receive a formal in-app notice detailing the reason and providing support contact information. Users may appeal any suspension by contacting us directly.

10 Third-Party Services

Hishab Potro integrates with the following third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Firestore): firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Drive API: policies.google.com/privacy
  • WhatsApp / IMO / SMS: Sharing is initiated by you directly. We do not transmit data to these platforms on your behalf

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services beyond our stated integrations.

11 Data Retention

Your local ledger data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Google Drive backup data is retained until you manually delete it from your Google Drive. Firebase authentication records are retained until you request account deletion. We do not retain any financial or customer data on our own servers.

12 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you via an in-app notification and update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.
